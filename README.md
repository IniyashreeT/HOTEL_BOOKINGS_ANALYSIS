🏨 Hotel Bookings Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
Overview

The Hotel Bookings Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive analysis of hotel booking performance. It enables users to monitor key business metrics, analyze booking trends, evaluate profitability, and gain insights into customer behavior through interactive visualizations and dynamic filters.

Key Features
Interactive dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI.
KPI cards displaying:
Total Customers
Total Revenue
Total Profit
Total Quantity (Bookings)
Average Customer Rating
Booking status distribution (Completed, Cancelled, Pending).
Year-wise profit analysis.
Revenue versus profit comparison.
Quantity analysis by sub-category.
Geographic map visualization of booking locations.
Top 10 cities ranked by revenue.
Interactive slicers for:
Region
Month
State
Dashboard Insights
Track overall hotel performance using key business metrics.
Analyze booking status to identify cancellation and completion trends.
Evaluate yearly profit growth and business performance.
Identify the highest revenue-generating cities.
Compare revenue and profit across different business segments.
Visualize booking distribution using an interactive map.
Filter reports dynamically for detailed regional and time-based analysis.

Business Value

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

Monitor hotel booking performance in real time.
Identify profitable markets and revenue-generating locations.
Understand booking patterns and customer trends.
Improve operational and strategic decision-making.
Generate actionable insights through interactive business intelligence reports.
Dashboard Components
KPI Cards: Total Customers, Revenue, Profit, Quantity, and Average Rating.
Donut Chart: Booking Status Distribution.
Treemap: Quantity by Sub Category.
Column Chart: Profit by Year.
Scatter Plot: Revenue vs Profit Analysis.
Map Visualization: Global Booking Distribution.
Bar Chart: Top 10 Cities by Revenue.
Slicers: Region, Month, and State.
